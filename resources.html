Resources

The Cooperative Open Online Landslide Repository (COOLR) is part of a larger landslides team effort at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center to learn where and when landslides occur. Read more about our publications related to COOLR, COOLR in the news, our landslide team projects, and find more information about landslides in general below.
